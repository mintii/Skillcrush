All About Me

How did I get interested into web development?

As my experience grew at my current job, I realized there's a huge need in technology, especially a need to reach out to small businesses. My experience with small businesses show that their websites are hardly up to par with what is needed to excel and attract potential customers.

I began to see great community groups pop up like Girl Develop It Ann Arbor, that encourage women to learn and advance in technological careers. I began to turn my self-doubt into fuel that this could actually work!

With the help of Girl Develop It Ann Arbor, I've learned many amazing skills so far.

    I have learned:
  • Creating my own WordPress blog
  • HTML and CSS
  • User Experience testing and research
  • Git and Github
  • Java

They deserve so much praise for the work they provide in our community. Girl Development actually has many many chapters around the whole United States! If you're reading this and feeling inspired, I recommend checking out which chapter is closest to you!
